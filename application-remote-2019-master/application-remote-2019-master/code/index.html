A small guide to Scrum

Scrum is a form of strategy used by a team when developing a product in an efficient and creative way within a time frame. It makes it easy to identify obstacles and to assign tasks on a daily basis. The framework consists of a Product Owner, a Development Team and a Scrum Master. The different roles makes it possible to have an agile team with the necessary knowledge to solve unseen problems within it.
